Linus signed this for me at LinuxWorld 1999 featureed in [Revolution OS](./watch/index.html) right after RMS received the Linus award: “So, very ironic things have happened, but nothing to match this. Giving the Linus Torvalds Award to the Free Software Foundation is sort of like giving the Han Solo Award to the rebel fleet.” Notice the doodle on the left hand side of the plate and the arrow pointing to it with Linus’s inscription “Happy Linuxing from Patricia 2.5 yr”. During the scene with RMS you can see his two children playing on the stage with their dad.
